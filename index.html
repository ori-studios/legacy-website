Welcome to Legacy SMP!

In our minecraft server, we try to turn Modern Minecraft into a nostalgic throwback to the old console edition!

Like there used to be on the Xbox 360, Playstation, Nintendo Switch, the Wii U and more!

Over time the SMP will expand to further push the recreation of the old versions!


The features we have on the Legacy SMP are:


Old Textures!

Play with the original textures of the game!

Old Style Combat!

Fight mobs, players and more with old-styled PvP!

Old World Terrain!

Revisit the old pre 1.18 terrain generation!

Bedrock Support!

Play alongside bedrock players!

Friendly Community

Play with a unique and friendly community!

Rule 1

No Stealing/griefing - This includes any form of stealing or griefing from other members


Rule 2

No cheats/hacks/eploits - This includes mods that include a huge advantage over vanilla players (i.e. freecam, abp and other such mods).


Rule 3

No hateful speech/language -- This includes, but is not limited to: slurs, discriminatory insults, etc


Rule 4

No spam/advertising - In game chat, you are not allowed to advertise: social links, discord servers, or minecraft servers


Rule 5

No NSFW content/conversations - This includes, but is not limited to: porn, nudity, gore, extreme violence, etc


Rule 6

No alt accounts/ban evasion - To allow server moderation to remain easy for staff, and to ensure nobody is ban evading, alt accounts are not allowed.


Rule 7

No Pvp without consent - Only PvP with other players if they consent to it, don't kill people without asking them.
